The conviction

If the default is broken, we fix it at runtime.

Enterprise platforms ship interfaces that respect neither your time nor your cognitive load. Tickets pile up. Roadmaps slip. Defaults stay terrible.

ShadoLogiq Labs builds userscripts, browser extensions, and embeddable toolkits that override those defaults on the client side — no central approval, no server dependencies, no agenda beyond the craft.
