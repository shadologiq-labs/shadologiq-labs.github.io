Gapps Embed

Embed Google apps anywhere.

A lightweight, themeable toolkit for embedding a Google apps grid on any site. One iframe, no build step, zero tracking. Three presentations: launcher, spotlight, and waffle menu.

SNapp Extension

ServiceNow, liberated.

A context-aware browser extension for ServiceNow forms. Extracts links into a sidebar, surfaces email contacts, and gives you a right-click menu for richly formatted record links. Chrome, Edge, Firefox — relaunching under ShadoLogiq Labs soon.
